Our Story

Home

In 1990, the late Mr. Ong Teng Cheong, then Secretary-General of NTUC, proposed developing a golf and country club to make golfing accessible and affordable for Singaporean workers.
His vision was to show that the progress of Singapore is shared by all, ensuring that golf would no longer be a game reserved for executives.

Key milestones

Phase 2

The second phase comprised the development of the Vanda and Dendro Courses, the Social Clubhouse and Orchid Golf & Resort Hotel (then Orchid Lodge). Most of Phase II was completed by the end of 1994. By the fourth quarter of 1995, the Dendro Course and the Bowling Alley were opened for play.
