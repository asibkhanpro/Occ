Driving Range

Home

The 160-bay driving range is one of the biggest driving ranges in South East Asia. It features a state-of-the-art ball automatic tee-up system activated with a smart card, consisting of two levels with 80 computerised tees at the sheltered lower level and 80 manual tees on the upper level. 
The only auto-tee system in Singapore!

Dress code

Golfers are encouraged to wear golfing attire and golfing/sports shoes. Sleeveless t-shirts (e.g
. singlets, tank tops & jerseys), jeans or running shorts are not allowed.
