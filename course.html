Along the waterways

Located along the truly picturesque waterways of the Sungei Seletar Reservoir, OCC boasts one of the most breathtaking golf courses in Singapore.

The Club boasts a unique 27-hole international championship golf course, designed with the guidance of the late former President of Singapore, Mr. Ong Teng Cheong. Named after orchids, the Aranda, Vanda, and Dendro courses feature meticulously manicured fairways and greens.
